For the first time in the world, Spotify is hosting it's first music festival, and we're calling it Spotify's Freewill Festival. This upcoming annual event will be featuring big stars like Halsey, CHVRCHES, Coldplay, and more. So sign up to our newsletter to be the first to attend this 1 day event, or you'll regret it! The festival will be taking place in the Polo Field, Lindley Meadow, and Hellman Hollow, which are all located at Golden Gate Park in San Francisco, California. It will be held on May 19 and 20 of 2017.
